[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Here's how it works under the hood. 
First, a human provides their emergency via voice or text through the FastAPI backend. 
That input goes directly to the Orchestrator Agent, which acts as the core triage unit to determine severity and the required response type. 
Instead of bottlenecking, the Orchestrator simultaneously routes to specialized sub-agents: the Ambulance Agent, Fire Agent, Police Agent, and Hospital Coordinator. 
At the exact same time, it routes to a unique Instruction Agent, which formulates real-time first aid steps. 
All of these agents write their actions to a shared state, which is streamed directly back to the human as a live simulation timeline.
